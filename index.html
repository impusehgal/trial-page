What is Tarot Card Reading?

The ultimate guide on how to do a basic three card spread reading

figcaption

What is Tarot? Simply defined, Tarot cards are decks of cards with pictures, symbols, and numbers printed on them. In theory, Tarot cards are used for divination and can give insight into your past, present, and future.To many, Tarot reading may seem supernatural and intimidating to approach. However, Tarot cards can be quite simple and offer a different perspective to a difficult situation or question you may have. Additionally, reading Tarot cards can be simply a fun hobby.

The tarot deck is made up of 78 cards divided into the Major Arcana and the Minor Arcana.

  • Major Arcana

    They are the labeled cards and represent principles, concepts, and ideas, and are typically more significant.

  • Minor Arcana

    These cards are divided into four suits and represent the details of the situation described by the Tarot spread.

The following table mentions cards that are most likely to be seen in a basic three card general spread reading.

Name Type of Card
The Death, Major Arcana King of Wands, Minor Arcana
